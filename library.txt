Library of Secrets

Located in the heart of the ancient city, the Library of Secrets is a treasure trove of arcane knowledge. It is said that within its dusty shelves, one can find maps and scrolls that hold the key to unimaginable power.

- Entrance: Hidden behind a bookcase in the main hall.
- Key Items: Ancient maps, magical tomes, and cryptic scrolls.
- Challenges: Puzzles and traps designed to deter the unworthy.
